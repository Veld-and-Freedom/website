Our Story

Born from
the bush.

Veld and Freedom was born from a deep-rooted love for Africa and its untamed spirit. Our family's connection to the African bush spans generations — instilling in us a profound respect for the land and its incredible biodiversity.

This brand is our way of sharing that passion. A platform to celebrate the beauty of nature, and to advocate for its conservation.

We believe fashion can and should be a force for good. Our focus is on durability and sustainability — high-quality garments built to last, that prove you don't have to choose between style and conscience.

Made in South Africa
